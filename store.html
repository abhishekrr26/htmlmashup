Welcome to Bean & Brew

Welcome to Bean & Brew Rest — your cozy corner for great coffee, simple food, and relaxed company. Step inside to the scent of freshly pulled espresso and warm pastries, a calm space made for lazy mornings, focused work sessions, or catching up with friends. Our baristas craft each drink with care, from creamy lattes and single-origin pour-overs to seasonal specials; our kitchen serves a small rotating menu of house-baked goods, hearty sandwiches, and thoughtful vegetarian and vegan options.

Whether you’re here to savor a quiet moment alone, meet up with friends, or get some work done, we’ve created a welcoming space for you. Our cozy seating, free Wi-Fi, and friendly staff make Bean & Brew the perfect spot to relax, connect, and enjoy great coffee and food. We look forward to welcoming you soon!

We believe in hospitality that feels like home: friendly service, reliable Wi‑Fi, plenty of plug-accessible tables, and a playlist that keeps the mood bright but unobtrusive. Whether you’re here for a quick takeout, a long catch-up, or your new favorite work spot, Bean & Brew Rest is here to welcome you
